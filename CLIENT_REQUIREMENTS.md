## 6. Payments

  - Bank transfer
- [ ] **Do you have a business bank account** set up to receive money?
- [ ] **Do you have a registered business / KRA PIN?** (Required for M-Pesa Paybill and most payment processors.)
- [ ] **Pricing in KES or USD?** Or both?
- [ ] **Will you pay instructors a share** of course sales? If yes, what percentage?
